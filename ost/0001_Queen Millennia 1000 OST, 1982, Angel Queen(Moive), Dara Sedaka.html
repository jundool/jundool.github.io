Queen Millennia 1000 OST, 1982, Angel Queen(Moive), Dara Sedaka

Queen Millennia is a manga series by Leiji Matsumoto which was serialized from
28 January 1980 through 11 May 1983 The 1982 film serves as a retelling of the anime series.
The 1999 setting is designed as more futuristic in appearance than in the TV series.
in addition, the film provides an alternate ending to the story leaving no room for the events
of the Maetel Legend OVA. The music score for the film was written and performed by Kitaro,
while the end-credits music was sung by American singer Dara Sedaka.
