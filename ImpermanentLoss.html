When depositing two coins into a liquidity pool, this calculator provides an estimate
of the impermanent loss incurred.
(not taking into account fees and other variables).
Just before depositing into the liquidity pool, you can check the results by entering
the quantities of coins A and B, the current dollar price, the subsequently
changed dollar price, and then clicking
the 'Calculate' button. In a liquidity pool, the ratio adjusts closer to 1:1 in response
to price movements. As the dollar value of each coin changes and the quantity
of coins fluctuates, impermanent losses occur due to price fluctuations upon
depositing into the liquidity pool. These results are expressed as final dollar values.
