List Description

When you access the Internet, you may be at a loss as to what to choose from the abundance of information.
I would like to remember the music I heard on the radio a long time ago and list my personal favorites one by one using the knowledge I naturally acquired while collecting albums and music.
I thought about linking the list directly, but since copyright issues may arise, I will continuously update the list and provide a brief description of each OST.
If you search YouTube using this list, you will be happy to be able to enjoy music anytime, anywhere.
